SKIN OF THE EARTH

Info Images
June 2–8th 10am–5pm
Late: June 5th 6–8pm
Three Rooms 74 Beulah Rd E17 9LE

A group show curated by Tim Green.

Skin of the Earth explores the porous boundaries between self, other, and land. Interrogating the uncanny border where the world ends and the human begins.

Featuring work by:

Abi Huxtable, Alex Fox, Aniela Murphy, Connor Doyle, Ellie Walker, Elspeth Vince, Emma Phillips, Karl Bielik, Lily Donaldson, Martha Hussey, Sharon Drew, Tim Green, & Unica Yabiku.
