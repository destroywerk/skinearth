Skin of the Earth

ThreeRooms Gallery. June 2 — 8. Curated by Tim Green

‘Skin Of The Earth’ is a group show bringing together fourteen artists to explore the porous boundaries between self, other, and land — interrogating the uncanny border where the world ends and the human begins.

Featured artists: Abi Huxtable, Alex Fox, Aniela Murphy, Connor Doyle, Ellie Walker, Elspeth Vince, Emma Phillips, Karl Bielik, Lily Donaldson, Martha Hussey, Sharon Drew, Stephen Keane, Tim Green, and Unica Yabiku.
